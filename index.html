Product´s info

Malnutrition is a very serious prpblem that needs to talk about consiering that there are many people that daily suffer of hunger and economic problems, as well of an extremely vulnerable situation. Sunrise Bar will be working by containing enough calories in order to not suffer from malnutrition. It counts with two differnet flavors, salty and sweet. Even though there are two types of bars, both of them have as main ingredient PUFFED RICE. This product will be packed in a box, each containing 6 bars, it could be either 6 salty bars, 6 sweet bars, or 3 sweet bars ans 3 salty bars. Since our product is based on th UN second goal, Zero Hunger, and we want to make it as accessible s possible for tose how need it, the Sunrise Bar could be either purchased at any store, or be delivered completely free to those families or areas that are most vulnerable in both poverty and hunger.

The sweet version of the bar contains a variation of ingredients, such as puffed rice, chickpea, almonds, nuts, peanuts, honey, chocolate(dark), quinoa, and sunflower seeds. As for the salty version of the bar it contains puffed rice, quinoa, chickpea, garlic powder, sunflower seeds, and other ingredients that provide the bar with the nutrients and calories needed in order to someone to consume what needed during a day.
